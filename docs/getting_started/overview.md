# Overview

WINPACT provides a modular lifecycle simulation of a wind asset, combining:

- **Metocean environment** — wind, wave, and site conditions driving the model.
- **Wind farm model** — layout, turbines, and coupling to PyWake for wake effects.
- **Response framework** — turbine-level mechanical and electrical response.
- **Market environment** — electricity price signals and dispatch logic.
- **Economic engine** — CAPEX, OPEX, financing (FINEX), revenue, cash flow, and valuation (LCOE, NPV, IRR).
- **Lifecycle modules** — lifetime extension (LTE), curtailment studies, and LCA.

The outputs support both **technical** (loads, fatigue, availability) and
**economic** (LCOE, NPV) decision-making.
