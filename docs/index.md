# WINPACT

**WINPACT** is a Python-based lifecycle modeling framework for wind energy
technologies, developed by the Integrity and Reliability Section of
DTU Wind and Energy Systems. It simulates operational performance and
couples metocean, technical, and economic factors to evaluate the effect
of design, control, and market decisions on both economic metrics
(LCOE, NPV) and technical health indicators.
